# 🌍 AI Global Trade Platform: Demographic & Economic Market Analysis

## 🎯 Executive Summary
This project establishes the data foundation for an AI-driven global trade platform. By integrating and analyzing massive datasets from the U.S. Census Bureau, UN Comtrade, and the OECD, this project uncovers actionable macroeconomic trends. The analysis challenges conventional market expansion strategies by proving that population size does not equate to market wealth, while also isolating regional supply chain shifts, specifically within Ohio's export sectors. 

## 🛠️ Technical Stack & Methodology
* **Core Technologies:** R (tidyverse, tidycensus, sf, tigris), APIs (Census, UN Comtrade, OECD).
* **Data Engineering:** Automated API data extraction, spatial data joining, and demographic rate normalization across millions of global and domestic trade records.
* **Advanced Modeling:** Developed and evaluated Multiple Linear Regression models (Full vs. Reduced) to predict state-level median household income based on socioeconomic indicators, optimizing for RMSE and R-squared values.

## 📊 Strategic Business & Marketing Insights
* **Precision Audience Targeting:** Statistical modeling revealed that raw population size is a poor predictor of market wealth. Instead, education rates and poverty metrics hold the highest predictive power, indicating that market entry and media planning strategies must prioritize socioeconomic targeting over mass-market reach.
* **Regional Business Intelligence:** Conducted localized analysis of Ohio's trade ecosystem, identifying a strategic pivot: while traditional automotive and electronics exports declined, high-value aerospace and defense sectors surged. 
* **Global Market Prioritization:** Mapped the concentration of global trade, identifying the U.S. and China as dominant import hubs, while isolating specific product sector demands (e.g., Machinery and Electrical Equipment) to inform targeted B2B marketing strategies.

## 💡 Translation to Analyst Roles
* **Business Analyst:** Translated abstract economic data into actionable market-entry strategies and regional supply chain assessments.
* **Data Analyst:** Demonstrated full-cycle data processing, from complex API extraction to geospatial mapping and predictive regression modeling.
* **Marketing & Media Analyst:** Applied statistical rigor to audience segmentation, proving that effective budget allocation requires targeting localized economic health indicators rather than broad population metrics.
